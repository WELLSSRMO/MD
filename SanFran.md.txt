### San Francisco Cocktail

**Ingredients**
* 1oz sloe gin
* 1oz dry vermouth
* 1 oz sweet vermouth
* dash of organge bitters
* dash of aromatic bitters
* cherry

**Steps**
1. Pour ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice
2. Shake well
3. Strain into chilled cocktail glass
4. Top with a cherry
