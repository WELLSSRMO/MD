### Coke Float

**Ingredients**
* 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
* 1/2 can of Coca-Cola, or a whole can, I'm not your mother

**Steps**
1. Scoop the ice cream
2. Drop in a glass
3. Fill with Coca-Cola to taste
4. optional: Top with a cherry and whip cream
